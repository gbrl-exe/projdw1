ABOUT

The Yale School of Art is a graduate school that confers MFAs in Graphic Design, Painting/Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture. And offers undergraduate-level art courses to Yale College students. Our website exists as an ongoing project of students of IFSP in web development. It functions as a wiki—all members of the School of Art community but dosn't have the ability to add new, and edit most existing content, cause one of the autor thinks that idea can't lead to anything "user friendly".
